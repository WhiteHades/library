# Hinduism

This idea hub collects distilled notes on Hindu thought, scripture, philosophy, social structure, etc. This page is the canonical public overview for the `hinduism` idea.

The current featured series comes from [[00_publish/hinduism/01_hinduism_and_the_universal_claim\|DawahWise's "Universal Truth" discussion]]. Future notes may come from multiple channels while still living physically inside this one `hinduism` publish folder.

The publishing system is designed so that notes can stay physically grouped under one idea folder while still being browsed publicly through richer layers like `channel` and `series`. That means this idea hub should eventually help a reader do three different things without friction:

1. understand the broad idea itself
2. move through major series in a clear reading order
3. discover how different channels approach the same subject

At the moment, the main public entry under this idea is the DawahWise series on whether Hinduism can sustain a universal truth claim. As the library grows, this hub will become the stable landing page for all Hinduism related notes, regardless of which channel or series they came from.
